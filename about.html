Our Story

Established in 1892, Henro Hotel was originally a royal retreat for European nobility. Over the decades, it has transformed into a world-class luxury destination while preserving its rich heritage. Our hotel has hosted legendary artists, royalty, and visionaries, creating an atmosphere of elegance and inspiration.
